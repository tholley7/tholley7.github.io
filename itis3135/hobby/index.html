About Me

I am originally from the Boston, MA area before moving to Charlotte, NC. Now, I am a sophomore studying Human-Computer Interaction at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte. I have been studying at this unversity for 2 years and am set to graduate in May of 2024. At UNC Charlotte, I serve as the Marketing Director for Niner Esports.

When not working, studying, or taking photos, you can almost certainly find me playing any one of several PC games including Rainbow Six: Siege, Apex Legends, and Call of Duty: Modern Warfare!

Travel

Until COVID-19 changed the world's travel industry, I travelled with my family and friends all around the united states. I also travelled to Spain as part of my school's Spanish Exchange program. I loved taking pictures of the country's unique landscape, people, and architecture.

In my domestic travels, I have visited and photographed landscapes in over 35 US States including Alaska, Southwest National Parks, and various spots on the coast from Virginia Beach to Malibu. Each new location offered a beautiful setting to capture.

Background

I first picked up a camera when my mom asked me to take pictures with her camera at one of my brother's baseball games when I was in middle school. Needless to say I was hooked. I started to use my mother's Nikon D3000 more and more until it eventually became more my camera than hers.

Since then, I have been all around the country as well as abroad to Spain's beautiful Basque Country. There, I was able to experience a whole new culture and document it along the way.
